About Us

CancerQuest is an AI-based website that provides information on various types of cancer, including causes, risk factors, prevention, and diagnosis. It offers probable cancer prediction and doctor referrals if desired. The website has the potential to transform cancer healthcare by improving early detection, personalized treatment, drug discovery, remote monitoring, and predictive analytics, leading to improved outcomes for cancer patients and reduced impact on communities affected by the disease.

Accessibility

CancerQuest provides accessible information on cancer causes, risks, and prevention.

Personalization

CancerQuest offers personalized prediction and doctor referrals for cancer patients.

Innovation

CancerQuest's AI-based approach can improve cancer healthcare outcomes.

Impact

CancerQuest can improve cancer outcomes and benefit communities
